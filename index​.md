# Privacy Policy — PDF Combiner

**Last updated:** March 20, 2026

## Summary

PDF Combiner does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

## Data Collection

PDF Combiner collects **no data** of any kind:

- No personal information
- No usage analytics or telemetry
- No crash reports sent to any server
- No advertising identifiers
- No tracking of any kind

## Files You Process

Files you import (images and PDFs) are processed entirely on your device.
They are never uploaded, shared, or transmitted anywhere.

## App Preferences

PDF Combiner stores your settings (export quality, page format, language)
locally on your Mac. This data never leaves your device.
